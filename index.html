About

Hi, I'm Yi (Alicia) Sun. I am a PhD student at MIT IDSS. I'm working with Kalyan Veeramachaneni in the Data to AI Lab at LIDS. My research interests are designing sequential algorithms that are robust and reliable as well as aligned with societal values. Before coming to MIT, I completed my undergraduate studies in Computer Science at Washington University in St. Louis, working with Kilian Q. Weinberger .
